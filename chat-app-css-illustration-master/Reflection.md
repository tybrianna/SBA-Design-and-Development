This project was definitely challenging! From the different divs you had to create to figuring out how to create the shapes in the background, it was for sure a brain twister.

The goal of this project was to choose a project to replicate from Frontend Mentor. I chose the chat app project. I chose this one because it looked more eye appealing and simple to replicate, I was wrong. Even though it was challenging, it taught me a lot.

Some challenges I faced were recreating the objects in the background, getting everything to fit inside the phone shaped box, and getting everything to align like in the original. To help me through those challenges I looked back through our lessons for tips that I might've missed. I also looked through MDN, W3 schools, and the readme.md that was provided in the file. Luckily the file included the images and styles needed for the project which made it easier. It also prevented me from wasting time on trying to get the colors to match because it was already provided to me.

Some improvements I need to make are to definitely slow down and to triple check my work. Most of my errors were due to closing commands too soon or not at all, typos, and simply making sure the verbiage matched in the CSS and HTML. Other than that, I know that with time and more practice I will only get better and better.
